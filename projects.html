Hydraulically Actuated Tube Bender Retrofit

Summary: Retrofitted a manual tube bender used in Boise State’s shop with a hydraulic ram and mobile frame to enable single‑operator bends, reduce effort, and improve safety.

My Role: Hand calculations for force/stress & component sizing; SolidWorks 3D modeling & drawings; MIG welding; plasma cutting; assembly & testing.

Tools: SolidWorks, manual calculations, MIG welding, CNC plasma, manual mill & lathe.

  • Before: floor‑bolted, high manual effort (sometimes 3 people + 12ft cheater bar), difficult to relocate.
  • After: hydraulic actuation; mobile frame moved by one person; dramatically improved ease of use and safety.

VARScent — VR Scent Delivery Attachment

Summary: Designed a swappable 4‑scent delivery module integrated with a VR headset to evaluate olfactory memory for potential Alzheimer’s diagnosis and longitudinal tracking.

My Role: Sole engineer on a multi‑disciplinary team (bio/chem/software). End‑to‑end design and implementation of the scent module.

Tools: Arduino microcontroller, C++, 3D printing/CAD, ultrasonic atomizers.

  • VR experience shows 4 objects; device delivers a scent vapor. Eye‑tracking measures recognition time vs. normative baselines or patient history.
  • Housings/cartridges are swappable and refillable; Arduino exposes simple commands (scent ID, duration).
